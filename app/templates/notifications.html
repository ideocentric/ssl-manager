How it works
  • A check runs once daily (08:00 server time) via a systemd timer.
  • If any certificates are expiring within the configured window, a single digest email is sent listing all of them sorted by days remaining.
  • No email is sent on days when nothing is expiring within the window.
  • Email delivery requires SMTP to be configured under Settings → Email.
